Actions around the world Register your Action

Safeguarding biodiversity calls for the active involvement of all citizens, businesses, governments and organizations. Collectively, we need to build a vast network to communicate the importance of biodiversity with the rest of world. Very substantial changes from business-as-usual trends are needed in order to address the challenges we face: to slow and stop the loss of biodiversity. 

The map below and the calendar give an overview of the work being done around the world in the context of the United Nations Decade on Biodiversity. Actions include specific programmes and projects, policy changes, activities, events and more. If you would like your action included, please register it by filling the form or contact us.

Cancun Commitments and Coalitions for Enhanced Implementation

Beginning at the UN Biodiversity Conference in Cancun, in 2016, Parties and other Governments, in concert with partner organizations and other entities, are invited to announce commitments or actions in support of the Convention, the Strategic Plan for Biodiversity 2011-2020, and the Aichi Biodiversity Targets.
